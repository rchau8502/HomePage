Process

How I would think through a real version later

1. Clarify the purpose

Decide what the company actually is, who it serves, and what belongs on the first screen.

2. Set the visual system

Rework typography, color, and supporting visuals around a real brand instead of a placeholder identity.

3. Replace concept content

Swap in actual work, contact expectations, and a truthful offer before treating the page like a live company site.
